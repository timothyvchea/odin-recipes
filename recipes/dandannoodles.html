Dan Dan Noodles

What is Dan Dan Noodles?

Dan Dan Noodles is another Sichuan dish. It is a dry noodle dish using a sesame based sauce. Its nutty and spicy sauce is delicious.

If you liked my Mapo Tofu dish, you will also like this one.

Ingredients

  • Chinese Wheat Noodles
  • Ground Pork
  • Chinese Five Spice
  • Hoisin Sauce
  • Chinese Preserved Cabbage
  • Sesame Paste
  • Soy Sauce
  • Chili Oil
  • Garlic
  • Spring Onion
  • Cucumbers
  • Sichuan Peppercorns

Steps

  1. Start by chopping the garlic and white parts of the spring onions into small diced pieces.
  2. Slice the cucumbers into thin slices.
  3. Put the noodles into boiling water, cooking it to the time it says on the packaging.
  4. Put a wok onto a hot stove and pour in cooking oil.
  5. Put in the ground pork, breaking it up into smaller pieces.
  6. Brown the ground pork, then put in the preserved cabbage.
  7. Pour in hoisin sauce and the five spice into the pork and cabbage mixture.
  8. In a seperate bowl, mix up the sesame paste, soy sauce, and Chili oil.
  9. Strain out the noodles and put on top of the sauce.
  10. Pour the pork mixture on top of noodles.
  11. OptionalRoast the peppercorns then grind them to use as a garnish.
  12. Use the green parts of the spring onion and the cucumbers as garnish
  13. Done!
