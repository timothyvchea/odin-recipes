Kimchi Cucumbers

What are Kimchi Cucumbers?

Kimchi is a style of preserving (pickling) vegetables in Korea. This dish is specifically preserving cucumbers in that style

Ingredients

  • Cucumbers
  • Salt
  • Garlic
  • Gochugaru (Korean Red Chili Powder)
  • Rice Vinegar
  • Soy Sauce
  • Sesame Oil
  • Sugar
  • OptionalOther vegetables

Steps

  1. Slice the cucumbers into medium-thickness rounds. If you have other vegetables, cut them into small strips.
  2. Salt the vegetables and let sit for about 20 minutes.
  3. Make the sauce by combining diced garlic, the Gochugaru, rice vinegar, soy sauce, sesame oil, and sugar.
  4. Mix thoroughly until the sugar is dissolved.
  5. Drain the vegetables from their salt mixture.
  6. Put vegetables into a container.
  7. Pour the sauce mixture into the container, mixing thoroughly.
  8. Let sit overnight.
  9. Done!
